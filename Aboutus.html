About Loving Homes

Our Story

Loving Homes was created with one mission in mind: to provide dogs with the comfort, love, and safety they deserve. What began as a small family-run service has grown into one of the most trusted luxury dog hotels in the region.

Over the years, we’ve welcomed thousands of pets through our doors, each receiving personalised care, premium accommodation, and the warmth of a second home. Our passionate team ensures your dog enjoys a fun, relaxing, and enriching experience — every single visit.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver a world-class experience for pets by combining professional care, modern facilities, and a genuine passion for animals. At Loving Homes, every dog is treated like family.

What Makes Us Different?

  • A fully trained pet-care team
  • Luxury suites and safe outdoor play areas
  • 24/7 on-call veterinary support
  • Grooming, spa treatments & personalised care programs
